Executive Summary

The YieldMax™ MSTR Option Income Strategy ETF (MSTY) is an actively managed, single‑issuer ETF that seeks to generate high monthly income by writing (selling) call options on shares of MicroStrategy Incorporated (MSTR), while collateralizing positions with cash and U.S. Treasurys. Launched on February 21, 2024, MSTY has delivered exceptional total‑return performance since inception—its NAV is up over 300% cumulative (annualized 225.5%) versus the S&P 500 TR at +13.6%. However, this comes with capped upside, full downside exposure to MSTR, elevated fees, and complex tax treatments.

1. Fund Mechanics & Strategy

  • Underlying Exposure: Indirect exposure to MSTR share price via a synthetic covered‑call strategy—MSTY writes both standardized and FLEX call options on MSTR, collecting premium while forgoing gains above the strike prices.
  • Collateralization: Premium‑backed by cash and short‑duration U.S. Treasurys to meet margin requirements and maintain liquidity.
  • Income Generation: Monthly distributions funded primarily from option premiums; secondary exposure to MSTR capital gains (capped).
